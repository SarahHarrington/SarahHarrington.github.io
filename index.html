Andrew Residence

Group Project | Prime Academy
The Andrew Residence is a residential mental health facility in Minneapolis.
They were using a paper binder for manually scheduling their on-call staff. Staff had to physically visit the facility to see if shifts were available and to verify if they had been scheduled. Supervisors had to manage emailing and calling staff when shifts needed to be urgently filled.
We were able to provide them with an online solution where supervisors can provide messages to staff, post available shifts, and confirm on-call staff shifts all while having the communications to staff generated automatically.
Staff can now log in to view shifts from anywhere, sign up and view their upcoming schedule. Weekly digest emails remind them to login to check the calendar.
Technologies: PostgreSQL, Express, AngularJS, Node.js, Angular Material, Moment.js, Plivo, Google Oauth 2.0
