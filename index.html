Andrew Residence

Andrew Residence On-call Staffing Solution
The Andrew Residence is a residential mental health facility in Minneapolis.
Andrew Residence had been using a paper process to manually schedule their 30 on-call staff members. The staff members had to physically visit the facility to see if shifts were available and to verify if they had been scheduled to work a shift. Supervisors had to manually manage emailing and calling staff when shifts needed to be urgently filled. We were able to provide them with an online solution.


Supervisors can now
  • Post messages for staff about things happening at the facility, training, or paperwork that needs to be completed
  • Post shifts for staff, including shifts that have a reoccurring need
  • Post shifts that have an urgent need to be filled, staff who have opted in will receive a text message notifying them of the shift

Staff can now
  • Log in to view shifts from anywhere
  • Sign up and view their upcoming schedule
  • Receive a weekly digest email with a summery of the available shifts reminding them to log in

Technology
PostgreSQL, Express, AngularJS, Node.js, Angular Material, Moment.js, Plivo, & Google Oauth 2.0
